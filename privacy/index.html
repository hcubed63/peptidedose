Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Educational & Research Use Only. Peptide Panel is a free educational tool designed to help individuals learn about peptide research. It is not a medical service, does not provide medical advice, and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition. All content is for informational and research purposes only. Users are encouraged to consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any peptide protocol.

1. Who We Are

Peptide Panel (peptidedose.netlify.app) is an independent educational web application. It provides a research information tool featuring AI-generated responses from simulated specialist personas, a dose calculator, weight tracker, and optional Google Calendar integration for tracking peptide dosing schedules.

This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to that data.

2. Data We Collect

Email address — collected on first visit via our entry form. Used to maintain a list of users who have acknowledged the educational nature of the tool. We do not use your email for marketing without your consent.

Blood results & health data (optional) — if you choose to enter blood markers, peptide stack, age, and name, this data is stored locally in your browser only (localStorage). It is never transmitted to our servers and is used solely to personalise AI panel responses within your session.

Weight entries & dose log (optional) — stored locally in your browser only. Never transmitted to our servers.

Google Calendar data (optional) — if you choose to connect Google Calendar, we request permission to create and read calendar events related to your peptide dosing schedule only. We do not read, store, or transmit any other calendar data. You can disconnect at any time.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Email addresses confirm users have acknowledged the educational and research-only nature of this tool
  • Health data is used only to personalise AI panel responses within your own browser session
  • Google Calendar access is used only to create and manage peptide dose reminder events you request
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing profiling

4. Google Calendar Integration

Peptide Panel offers optional integration with Google Calendar via OAuth 2.0. When you connect your Google Calendar:

  • We request access only to create and manage calendar events related to your peptide dosing schedule
  • We do not access, read, store, or share any other content from your Google account
  • You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account permissions page
  • Our use of Google API data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements

Peptide Panel's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Storage & Security

All personal health data (blood results, weight, dose logs) is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device, we cannot access it, and it is not backed up to any server.

Email addresses submitted via the entry form are stored securely via Netlify Forms on Netlify's infrastructure.

6. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We use browser localStorage to remember that you have acknowledged the disclaimer and to store your optional health data locally. This is not shared with any third party.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Netlify — hosts this website and collects form submissions. Netlify Privacy Policy
  • Google Calendar API — used only when you explicitly connect your calendar. Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI responses are generated via Anthropic's API. Your questions and optional health context are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate responses. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google Privacy Policy

8. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you (email address submitted on entry)
  • Request deletion of your email from our records
  • Disconnect Google Calendar access at any time
  • Clear all locally stored health data by clearing your browser's localStorage

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

Peptides discussed on this platform are for research purposes only and are not approved for human use in most jurisdictions.

Always consult a qualified and licensed healthcare professional before beginning any peptide or supplement protocol. The creators of Peptide Panel accept no liability for decisions made based on information provided by this tool.
